Class: 
"CityState"

Inherits class: 
"Cities"


New attributes: 
1. Sovereignty (some city-states, e.g. Gibraltar lack full sovereignty)
2. Currency
3. Number of constituing separate cities (under some definitions city-states may actually include more than one city)

New methods:
1. Public: give sovereignty to the city-state
2. Private: add another city to the city-state
