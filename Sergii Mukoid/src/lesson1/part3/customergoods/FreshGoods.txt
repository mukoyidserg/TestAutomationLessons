Class: 
"FreshGoods"

Inherits class: 
"CustomerGoods"


New attributes: 
1. Type of goods (e.g. vegetables, milk products)
2. Freshness percentage (percentage of shelf life left before shipment to the store)
3. Delivery type (cross-docked (supplier-to-store shipment) or stored (supplier-warehouse-store))

New methods:
1. Public: agree new freshness percentage with the supplier
2. Private: lose marketable condition
