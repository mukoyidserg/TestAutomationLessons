Class: 
"CompanyWorker"

Attributes: 
1. Age
2. The duration of work in the company
3. Years of experience
4. Salary
5. Position
6. Division (e.g. Logistics or Operations)
7. Number of subordinates
8. Relation to the company (outsource or in-house employee)
9. Company ownership (percentage of company shares owned)
10. Employment type (permanent or temporary)

Public methods:
1. Give a task to the employee
2. Increase employee's motivation
3. Change worker's position
4. Change worker's salary
5. Fire an employee

Private methods:
1. Take a vacation
2. Complete supervisor's task
3. Leave the company
4. Visit office
5. Clean working desk
