Class: 
"Cities"

Attributes: 
1. Population
2. Area
3. City's gross domestic product (GDP)
4. Number of divisions
5. Year of founding
6. Country
7. Name
8. Elevation (above the sea level, in meters)
9. Population growth rate
10. Population density

Public methods:
1. Change number of divisions
2. Introduce utilities
3. Introduce public transport
4. Facilitate population increase
5. Defend city from invaders (preserving city's country)

Private methods:
1. Change population size
2. Change area size
3. Develop economy (leading to GDP growth)
4. Come into decay 
5. Change country
